About

“InvestPro” Multiprrpose Wallet & Banking Online Investment Platform. It is a fully functional web Platform for managing your Wallet , Banking & Online investment platform conveniently. It allows you to register users, take deposits, remit earnings and process both single and multi-level ROI earnings. With InvestPro you’ll be able to setup your own platform as fast as possible. Major payment gateways and cryptocurrencies supported include Stripe, Coinpayments (BTC, ETH, LTC, DASH, XRP and BCH) and Paypal.

Creating a new Database

First thing you want to do before installing InvestPro is to create a new database on your mysql server. If you already know how to do this/or have already created one just skip to the next step.

Your hosting provider will most likely have phpMyAdmin as mysql manager. If that is the case here is a step by step guide (if not the process will be very similar on other managers.

Login to your control panel, find and click phpMyAdmin link.

Click on the database tab in the top menu, enter any name for your database and click create.

Uploading Files

After creating a database, unzip the file you donwloaded from CodeCanyon and upload the contents of InvestPro folder to your server root, usually called www or html or public_html.

Important: Always use a desktop application like FileZilla to upload files to FTP as opposed to web based file managers to avoid issues with corrupted or not properly uploaded files.
Important: Make sure that .htaccess file got copied properly from the download to main InvestPro folder on your server.

Investment Plan

In Investment Plan system, here user will invest from their balance in given Invest Plan/Packages. There is 2 type of invest management system.

  • 1. Fixed Investment
  • 2. ROI (Return Of Investment)

1. Fixed Investment : User will invest from his/her balance as "Fixed". He/She will get interest for "LIFETIME" of purchased plan.

2. ROI Investment : User will invest from his/her balance. And they will get interest as purchased plan.

Fund Transfer

In Fund Transfer system, here user will transfer balance from his/her account to another account of this project.

User will put receiver EMAIL ADDRESS then (transfer charge + Transfer Amount) will deduct from his/her balance. And finally "Transfer Amount" will add into receiver balance.
